At the beggining

Do you like Japanese food? I love Japanese food. Sushi, Ra-men, Japanese carry etc. Therefore, I went to various place in New Zealand and searched for a Japanese Restaurant. Anyway, I'm going to introduce many japanese restaurant that are my personal choice in here.
1, Fresh Sushi in Napier Rating:★★★★★

Fresh Sushi in the shooping street of Naiper. Fresh Sushi sells varius dishes as well as sushi. Japanese Caryy, Udon, Tonkatsu etc. Of course, the taste is very good. Even if you never eaten Japanese food, you will be happy. Among them, I think Udon is best in Fresh store.

2, Sushi Club in Napier Rating:★★★★

This restaurant also sells other Japanese food as well as sushi. A feature of this restaurant is that it is selling various types of sushi and other Japanese food. the rule is small sushi and other small things take by yourself, but you can order other udon and lunch boxes if you ask the clerk.
